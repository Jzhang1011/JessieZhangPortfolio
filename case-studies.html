KPI Operating System for Multi-LOB Marketing

Angle: Ops + Intelligence • Standardization + trust

Context

Multi-LOB reporting required consistent KPIs and repeatable executive readouts.

What was broken

  • Inconsistent definitions and manual processes created trust and accuracy risk.

What I did

  • Established KPI definitions, governance, QA steps, and a monthly operating rhythm.

What shipped

  • KPI dictionary + governance, dashboard index, MOR template.

Adoption

  • Embedded into rhythms; enabled stakeholders via documentation and support.

Outcomes

  • Improved exec confidence and reduced reporting risk.

Next

  • Expand automation and metric health monitoring.

Customer Journey Measurement Framework

Angle: Growth + Ops • Prioritize and optimize journeys

Context

Needed a structured way to prioritize journeys and focus optimization effort.

What was broken

  • Too many potential journeys; unclear KPI ownership and instrumentation requirements.

What I did

  • Built journey KPI mapping, prioritization criteria, and a repeatable optimization loop.

What shipped

  • Journey scorecard, KPI tree per stage, instrumentation checklist, test backlog template.

Adoption

  • Embedded into planning and readouts to keep focus on highest-leverage journeys.

Outcomes

  • Clearer prioritization and faster movement from insight to action.

Next

  • Layer in experimentation and incrementality by journey.
