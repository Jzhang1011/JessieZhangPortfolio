How I approach transformation work

1. Clarify the decision

I start by identifying the decision that needs to move faster or become more reliable, not just the metric that needs to be reported.

2. Build the operating layer

I translate fragmented data, roles, and workflows into a usable operating model with definitions, ownership, and governance.

3. Enable adoption

I design systems people can actually use — dashboards, templates, frameworks, or AI-enabled tools that support real workflows.

4. Scale knowledge

The goal is not just to solve one problem once, but to make expertise reusable and decision-making more scalable across the organization.
